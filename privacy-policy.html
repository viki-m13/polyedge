Privacy Policy

PolyEdge - Edge Finder | Last updated: January 2025

Summary: PolyEdge does not collect, store, or share any personal information. Your privacy is fully protected.

Information We Do NOT Collect

PolyEdge is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Device identifiers for advertising
  • Browsing history or app usage patterns

Information We Access

To provide our service, PolyEdge accesses:

  • Market Data: Publicly available prediction market data from Polymarket's API. This contains no personal user information.
  • Subscription Status: Anonymous subscription verification through RevenueCat to determine Pro feature access. This does not include any personally identifiable information.

Third-Party Services

PolyEdge uses the following third-party services:

  • RevenueCat: For subscription management. RevenueCat processes transactions anonymously. RevenueCat Privacy Policy
  • Apple App Store: For payment processing. Apple handles all payment information. Apple Privacy Policy
  • Polymarket API: For market data. We only access publicly available market information.

Data Storage

PolyEdge stores minimal data locally on your device:

  • Your watchlist selections (stored only on your device)
  • App preferences (stored only on your device)

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server.

Children's Privacy

PolyEdge is not intended for use by children under 17. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.
